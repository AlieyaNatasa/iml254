My memories





These memories are unforgettable because all my mother's side of the family can finally get together for the holidays. This rarely happens because everyone goes back to another village. My grandmother's extended family gathered and we had planned a picnic by the beach. This has strengthened the bond of brotherhood between us all
This is a precious memory. This picture is the last picture we took together before our ustaz or class teacher whom we loved very much passed away this year. This picture is a picture when taking SPM results. And this picture is also the last picture of our classmate took photo together before we parted to enter a new world of learning.
